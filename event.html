Code Buddy

Code Buddy is a code relay competition where the two members of the team have to code the solution for two different problem statements sitting in different labs. The real twist here is the exchange of the problem statements after some time and completing the solution which your partner has left midway. The contest will be hosted on HackerRank.

Rules and Regulations-
  1. Each team will consist of 2 members.
  2. Team members aren't allowed to communicate with each other during the contest.
  3. Use of Mobile phones,internet or any other electronic device is prohibited.
  4. Any type of comments in the code will lead to disqualification of the team.
  5. The contest duration is 60 minutes and the partners are required to exchange their seats at midtime.
  6. The decision of judges will be final and binding on all participants.

Team Distribution-
  1. FE/SE (Junior Team): Team of 2
  2. TE/BE (Senior Team): Team of 2
Fees: Rs.100 per team (Juniors/Seniors).

Bug Off

Bug Off is a one of its kind coding competition where the participants are expected to remove the bugs from the given set of codes and achieve the desired output within the time limit. The participant (Team/Individual) with maximum number of error-free codes wins the contest.

Rules and Regulations-
  1. Both the groups, FE-SE and TE-BE, will be given maximum 60 minutes to compete.
  2. FE-SE participants will be given a choice between C/C++/JAVA languages while TE-BE participants won't be having any such choice.
  3. The participants of FE-SE will be allowed to use the compiler for an interval of 5 minutes in every 20 minutes.
  4. The participants of TE-BE will be allowed to use the compiler for an interval of 10 minutes after every 20 minutes.
  5. Use of compilers in the time span other than the provided time interval will lead to disqualification of the team.

Team Distribution-
  1. FE-SE (Junior Team): Team of 2
  2. TE-BE (Senior Team): Team of 2
Fees: Rs.100 per team
